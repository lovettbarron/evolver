  - 10 jack-in: `jack-jf-identity-trig`, `jack-jf-2n-trig`, `jack-jf-3n-trig`, `jack-jf-4n-trig`, `jack-jf-5n-trig`, `jack-jf-6n-trig`, `jack-jf-time-in`, `jack-jf-intone-in`, `jack-jf-fm-in`, `jack-jf-run-in`

  - Bottom: RUN, FM, TIME, INTONE CV inputs + MIX output

- `midiToRotation(value: number): number` helper (same as Maths pattern)
- `JACK_POSITIONS` derived from CONTROL_POSITIONS filtering jack types
