**Step 1: Create Ikarie overview page** (`modules/ikarie/overview.md`)

2. **Heading**: `# Bastl Instruments Ikarie` with tagline `*Stereo dual-peak resonant filter with envelope follower*` and `**8HP** | Filter`
3. **Architecture**: Describe the stereo dual-peak filter — two filter cores that operate simultaneously, producing LP and HP simultaneously. CUTOFF sweeps from LP (CCW) to HP (CW) with center = open filter. The BEYOND output provides the spectral difference between the two filter cores — producing band-pass and twin-peak resonance effects. Two stereo modes: PAN (stereo panning based on STEREO knob) and SPREAD (frequency spread between the two filter cores). Built-in envelope follower (FOLLOW) that outputs CV proportional to input amplitude. **Critical normalling**: FOLLOW output is normalled to MOD input — without any cables, the filter automatically follows the input dynamics (auto-wah). Patching a cable to MOD breaks this normalling. VCA CV input controls output level (0-5V, no cable = full level). V/OCT tracks 1V/oct for musical filter sweeps.
4. **Controls Reference**: Table for all 18 controls. Highlight the FOLLOW-to-MOD normalling explicitly.
5. **Basic Patch (Init State)**: CUTOFF at noon (open filter), INPUT at unity (1x), RESONANCE at min, STEREO at noon (no effect), MOD at noon (no modulation — but FOLLOW normalling still active, so filter will gently follow dynamics), FOLLOW SPEED at MID, PAN/SPREAD at PAN. Patch audio source to L IN, L OUT to mixer.
6. **Patch Tips**: Brief list of the 8 patch tips from the manual (formant filtering, quad acid, beyond stereo, pinging, self-oscillation, post-filter ring mod, waveform crossfading, aggressive 24dB filtering).
7. **Further Reading**: Ikarie Manual (references/ikarie-manual.pdf), Bastl Instruments website.
