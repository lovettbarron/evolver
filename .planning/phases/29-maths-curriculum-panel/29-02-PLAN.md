**Section 2: Architecture** (heading: `## Architecture`, per D-05)
Write a 3-4 paragraph description covering:
- 4-channel structure: Ch1 and Ch4 are full-featured analog function generators (Rise, Fall, Vari-Response, Cycle). Ch2 and Ch3 are simple attenuverters
- Signal flow: Each channel processes or generates voltage. Ch1/Ch4 can be triggered externally or self-cycle (LFO/oscillator mode)
- OR/SUM/INV bus: OR outputs the highest voltage from all 4 channels. SUM adds all channels. INV inverts the SUM. These are always active -- free mixing/combining
- EOC/EOR: Ch1 outputs End-of-Rise (EOR) trigger. Ch4 outputs End-of-Cycle (EOC) trigger. These enable cascading envelopes, clock division, and timing logic
- Vari-Response: Continuously variable response curve from logarithmic through linear to exponential -- shapes how the voltage rises and falls

**Section 3: Controls Reference** (heading: `## Controls Reference`, per D-05)
Create 4 sub-sections: `### Channel 1`, `### Channels 2 and 3`, `### Channel 4`, `### Buses`.

Each sub-section uses this table format:

| Control | Type | Range | Function |
|---------|------|-------|----------|

Channel 1 table (14 rows):
- Rise | Knob | ~0.5ms to 25 min | Sets rise time of voltage function
- Fall | Knob | ~0.5ms to 25 min | Sets fall time of voltage function
- Vari-Response | Knob | Log to Exp | Shapes response curve (logarithmic to linear to exponential)
- Attenuverter | Knob | -1 to +1 | Scales and/or inverts channel output
- Cycle | Button + LED | On/Off | Enables self-cycling (LFO mode)
- Signal IN | Jack | -10V to +10V | Direct-coupled input for slew/lag/ASR
- Trigger IN | Jack | Gate/Pulse | Triggers envelope regardless of Signal IN
- Rise CV | Jack | CV | Voltage control of rise time
- Fall CV | Jack | CV | Voltage control of fall time
- Both CV | Jack | CV | Controls both rise and fall time simultaneously
- Cycle IN | Jack | Gate | External cycle enable/disable
- Unity OUT | Jack | 0-8V | Full-scale unattenuated output
- EOR OUT | Jack | Gate | End-of-rise trigger pulse
- Variable OUT | Jack | Scaled | Attenuverter-scaled output

Ch2/Ch3 table: Attenuverter (Knob, -1 to +1, Scales/inverts input), Signal IN (Jack, -10V to +10V, Input), Variable OUT (Jack, Scaled, Attenuverter-scaled output).

Ch4 table: Mirror of Ch1 table but with EOC OUT instead of EOR OUT (End-of-cycle trigger pulse).

Buses table: OR OUT (Jack, 0-8V, Highest voltage from all channels), SUM OUT (Jack, Bipolar, Sum of all channels), INV OUT (Jack, Bipolar, Inverted SUM).
