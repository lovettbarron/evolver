**Maths panel layout (20HP, tall eurorack format):**
The Maths panel is vertically oriented with a symmetrical left-right layout:
- Channel 1 occupies the LEFT side (Rise/Fall/Response knobs top, jacks bottom)
- Channels 2+3 (simple attenuverters) occupy the CENTER column
- Channel 4 occupies the RIGHT side (mirror of Ch1, with EOC instead of EOR)
- Bus outputs (OR/SUM/INV) are at the BOTTOM CENTER

**ViewBox:** Use `0 0 300 700` for a 20HP module (narrower and taller than Cascadia's 1000x580). The width of 300px represents 20HP. The height of 700px accommodates the vertically-stacked eurorack layout with knobs at top, jacks at bottom.

**Physical layout top-to-bottom:**

Row 1 (y ~40-100): Channel labels and Cycle buttons + LEDs
- Ch1 Cycle button+LED at left (~x:55)
