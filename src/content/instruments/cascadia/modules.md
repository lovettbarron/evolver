# Cascadia Hardware Modules

17 modules arranged left to right on the panel. This index follows the physical panel layout, which matches the manual's organization.

## Sound Sources

1. **[MIDI/CV](/instruments/cascadia/modules/midi-cv)** -- MIDI-to-CV converter with 8 assignable outputs (pitch, gate, velocity, trigger, CC, mod, LFO, clock). 4 buttons, 8 jacks.
2. **[VCO A](/instruments/cascadia/modules/vco-a)** -- Primary analog oscillator with through-zero FM, hard/soft sync, and pulse width modulation. 11 controls, 6 jacks.
3. **[VCO B](/instruments/cascadia/modules/vco-b)** -- Secondary analog oscillator, switchable to LFO rates. Four simultaneous waveform outputs. 5 controls, 6 jacks.

## Modulators

4. **[Envelope A](/instruments/cascadia/modules/envelope-a)** -- ADSR envelope generator with Hold stage, velocity sensitivity, and stage trigger outputs. 8 controls, 6 jacks.
5. **[Envelope B](/instruments/cascadia/modules/envelope-b)** -- Triple-mode function generator: Envelope, LFO, or Burst Generator. 9 controls, 6 jacks.
6. **[LFO X/Y/Z](/instruments/cascadia/modules/lfo-xyz)** -- Three rate-linked bipolar triangle LFOs with independent rate dividers and trimmers. 4 controls, 4 jacks.

## Shapers

7. **[Mixer](/instruments/cascadia/modules/mixer)** -- Blends VCO A waveforms, noise, sub-oscillator, and two patchable inputs with soft clip. 8 controls, 7 jacks.
8. **[VCF](/instruments/cascadia/modules/vcf)** -- Multimode voltage-controlled filter with 8 types (LP1/LP2/LP4, BP2/BP4, HP4, Notch, Phaser). Self-oscillating. 8 controls, 8 jacks.
9. **[Wave Folder](/instruments/cascadia/modules/wave-folder)** -- Waveform folding circuit for complex harmonic generation. 2 controls, 2 jacks.
10. **[VCA B / LPF](/instruments/cascadia/modules/vca-b-lpf)** -- Second VCA with 4-pole ladder low-pass filter, combinable as a Low Pass Gate. 3 controls, 4 jacks.

## Utilities

11. **[Line In](/instruments/cascadia/modules/line-in)** -- External audio input with level attenuator and clip indicator. 1 control, 1 jack.
12. **[VCA A](/instruments/cascadia/modules/vca-a)** -- Primary linear voltage-controlled amplifier with auxiliary input. 3 controls, 3 jacks.
13. **[Push Gate](/instruments/cascadia/modules/push-gate)** -- Manual gate button for triggering envelopes without MIDI or CV. 1 control, 1 jack.
14. **[Utilities](/instruments/cascadia/modules/utilities)** -- Sample & Hold, Slew Limiter / Envelope Follower, and Mixuverter in one strip. 8 controls, 9 jacks.
15. **[Patchbay](/instruments/cascadia/modules/patchbay)** -- Buffered Multiples, Summer, Inverter, Bipolar-to-Unipolar converter, Expression Source, and Ring Modulator. 1 control, 17 jacks.
16. **[FX Send/Return](/instruments/cascadia/modules/fx-send-return)** -- Effects loop for external pedals with send/return level, phase, and dry/wet mix. 5 controls, 2 jacks.
17. **[Output Control](/instruments/cascadia/modules/output-control)** -- Final output stage with drive, soft clip, and master level. 3 controls, 5 jacks.
