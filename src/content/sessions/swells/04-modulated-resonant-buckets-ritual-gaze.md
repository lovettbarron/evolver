# Session 04: Character Effects -- Buckets, Ritual & Gaze

**Objective:** Explore the modulated and resonant algorithms (Buckets, Ritual, Gaze) and build a patch that exploits each algorithm's unique modulation character.

> [!tip] If you only have 5 minutes
> Select Buckets. Set DECAY to 1 o'clock, EBB to 3 o'clock, FLOW to 2 o'clock. Play a simple repeating pattern. Hear the bucket-brigade chorusing in the reverb tail. That vintage character is what makes these three algorithms special.

## Warm-Up (2 min)

Init state. Select Velvet from Session 03 and play a note to remember where you left off. Then switch to Buckets to enter today's territory -- these algorithms are about character and modulation, not ambience.

## Setup

1. Init state (see [Swells Overview](/modules/swells/))
2. Audio source to **IN L**, stereo outputs to mixer
3. DECAY at **1 o'clock**, SIZE at **12 o'clock**, MIX at **1 o'clock**
4. Start with **Buckets** algorithm

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Buckets -- Analog Character (7 min)

<div data-swells-panel
  data-highlights="slider-swells-ebb:blue,slider-swells-flow:blue,slider-swells-decay:amber,slider-swells-size:amber"
></div>

1. Play a repeating pattern through Buckets. Notice the bucket-brigade-style chorusing in the reverb tail
2. Sweep **EBB** slowly -- in Buckets mode, this controls the analog-style modulation depth
3. Sweep **FLOW** -- this adds movement and warble characteristic of bucket-brigade circuits
4. Try extreme EBB + FLOW settings -- the reverb becomes a warped, pitch-shifting chorus
5. Reduce SIZE and increase DECAY -- notice how Buckets creates a dense, modulated space
6. Try feeding a rhythmic pattern -- Buckets adds analog character to the rhythm

### Exercise 2: Ritual -- Metallic Resonance (7 min)

<div data-swells-panel
  data-highlights="slider-swells-ebb:blue,slider-swells-flow:blue,slider-swells-hi-damp:amber,slider-swells-eq:amber"
></div>

1. Switch to **Ritual** with the same fader positions
2. Ritual creates metallic, resonant reverb -- listen for the ringing quality
3. Sweep **EBB** -- Ritual's EBB controls the resonant character
4. Sweep **FLOW** -- this shifts the resonant frequency content
5. Try reducing **HI DAMP** to minimum -- the metallic ringing becomes more pronounced
6. Sweep **EQ** -- Ritual responds dramatically to tonal changes
7. Feed a single percussive hit -- Ritual turns it into a metallic cascade

### Exercise 3: Gaze -- Deep Immersion (6 min)

<div data-swells-panel
  data-highlights="slider-swells-decay:amber,slider-swells-size:amber,slider-swells-ebb:blue,slider-swells-flow:blue"
></div>

1. Switch to **Gaze**. This algorithm is hypnotic and immersive
2. Increase **DECAY** to 3 o'clock and **SIZE** to 3 o'clock -- Gaze rewards long tails
3. Sweep **EBB** -- in Gaze mode, this creates deep, swirling modulation
4. Sweep **FLOW** -- the movement becomes trance-like
5. Set MIX to fully wet and play a slow melody -- listen to the hypnotic trails

### Exercise 4: Modulation Character Patch (3 min)

<div data-swells-panel
  data-highlights="slider-swells-ebb:blue,slider-swells-flow:blue,knob-swells-drive:amber"
></div>

1. Choose the algorithm you found most interesting (Buckets, Ritual, or Gaze)
2. Set EBB and FLOW to positions that create the most distinctive modulation
3. Add a touch of **DRIVE** to color the reverb input
4. Play your source material and find the sweet spot where the algorithm's character shines
5. Document this as your character effects patch

## What You Learned

- Buckets creates bucket-brigade-style analog modulation in the reverb
- Ritual produces metallic, resonant reverb textures
- Gaze generates hypnotic, deeply immersive reverb
- EBB and FLOW are the personality controls -- they define each algorithm's unique character

## Document Your Results

Record your character effects patch: algorithm, all fader positions, DRIVE setting, and source material. Compare this to your ethereal texture patch from Session 03 -- note how different these algorithms feel.
